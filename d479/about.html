About Taniti

Restaurants

Culinary Delights

Taniti is home to 10 diverse restaurants, offering options for every taste: five serve local fish and rice dishes, three specialize in American-style meals, and two provide delicious Pan-Asian cuisine. Whatever your craving, you'll find it here.

Grocery Stores

Local Markets

For visitors who prefer to cook, Taniti offers two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and a 24-hour convenience store to meet all your food and supply needs.

Lodging

Comfort and Variety

From cozy hostels to a luxurious four-star resort, Taniti offers a range of accommodations. Many family-owned hotels and bed & breakfasts ensure a personal and unique stay.

Entertainment

Activities for Everyone

Beaches, rainforests, and our iconic volcano are major attractions, but there's more: museums, fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining, pubs, a dance club, a movie theater, art galleries, an arcade, bowling, and more. Merriton Landing is the hub for many of these activities.

Sightseeing

Explore the Island

Whether you're wandering the streets of Taniti City with its native architecture, lounging on the white-sandy beaches, or taking in the majestic views of the active volcano, Taniti's sights are unforgettable.

Transportation

Getting to Taniti

Most visitors fly into Taniti's expanding airport, though cruise ships also dock weekly at Yellow Leaf Bay. We are continuously improving our transportation infrastructure to welcome more guests.

Ground Transportation

Getting Around

Public buses in Taniti City, taxis, rental cars, and bikes are available to help you explore the island. Merriton Landing and Taniti City are very walkable and easy to navigate.
